Odin Recipes | Pizza

Description

Pizza is the classic dish that quite literally everyone knows and loves. It a glance, its one of the easist dishes to assemble. A bread base covered with a tomato sauce, topped with cheese. Yet, this combination leaves a near infinite amount of possibliites with additional toppings.

Ingredients

  • 125 g buffalo mozzarella (drained)
  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 150 ml water (lukewarm)
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 3½ g active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt (divided)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • 150 ml tomato purée
  • 10 g basil
  • 50 g cherry tomatoes
  • flour, for work surface
  • basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add sugar and yeast to lukewarm water; stir thoroughly until dissolved.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix together flour and salt. Then add olive oil and water mixture to center of the flour and mix together until well incorporated.
  3. Turn out dough onto a floured work surface and knead until dough is smooth, but firm. Transfer back to floured bowl, cover with kitchen towel, and store in a warm, dry place for approx. 1 – 2 hours until dough has doubled in volume.
  4. Place pizza stone in unheated oven, then preheat to 250°C/480°F.
  5. Remove dough from bowl. On a floured work surface, flatten out to approx. the size of the pizza stone. Transfer to a pizza paddle.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together tomato purée, olive oil, and salt. Roughly tear up basil leaves and add them to sauce. Spread a thin layer over pizza, transfer to pizza stone, and bake in preheated oven at 250°C/480°F for approx. 6 – 7 min.
  7. Meanwhile, cut cherry tomatoes in half and buffalo mozzarella into thin slices. Remove pizza from oven; place tomatoes and mozzarella on top, return to oven and bake for another 6 – 7 min. until cheese is melted and crust is golden.
  8. Garnish pizza with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with high quality olive oil before serving, if desired. Enjoy!
