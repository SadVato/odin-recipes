Odin Recipes | Pasta Verde

Description

Pasta Verde takes thes tradtional american dish of spaghetti and adds a subtle, but powerful, mexican twist. Instead of the robust tomato based sauce that you maybe used to, this version of the dish uses an arugula based sauce that gives it a nice green tint.

Ingredients

  • 250 g long pasta (e.g. spaghetti)
  • 150 g Greek yogurt
  • 125 g arugula
  • 20 g basil
  • 50 g almonds
  • 60 g Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in plenty of salted, boiling water according to package instructions for approx. 10 â€“ 12 min. until al dente. Drain, save some pasta water and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, finely puree arugula, basil, almonds, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir herb mixture into Greek yogurt. Season again with lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta with yogurt-pesto. If the pasta seems too dry, add some of the previously saved pasta water. Serve with shavings of Parmesan cheese.
