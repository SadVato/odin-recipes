Odin Recipes | Steak

Description

Steak is the perfect emobidement of what it means to be american. Simple, powerful, strong. Afterall, a proper steak does not take much to perfect. Realistically, all you have to do is let the meat speak for itself. Just remember to never cook a steak well done because it'll be the last thing you ever eat. Respectfully.

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef minute steaks
  • 60 g butter
  • 3 loaves garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Season with salt and pepper. Let it rest for a bit.
  2. Place it on the well-heated pan with olive oil for about 3 minutes.
  3. Turn it over and add garlic with butter. Cook for another 3 to 4 minutes and glaze it with butter all the time.
  4. Take it off and serve with the rest of oil. You are good to go with your juicy medium rare steak!
